Pensieve

Capture thoughts by voice, organized by AI

Just say it. AI takes care of the rest.

Named after the Pensieve from Harry Potter — pour your thoughts in and revisit them later. The best way to take notes is to not write notes at all. Just speak.

🎙 Record on the Go

One tap, no typing. While walking, driving, or whenever inspiration strikes — just say a sentence.

📱 Offline Recognition

Built-in speech recognition model. No network needed. Recordings never leave your device.

✨ AI Organization

Auto-generated summaries and tags. Tap "Organize" and your scattered thoughts become structured text.

🔄 Append

Add follow-up recordings to the same thought. Fragmented thinking from different moments comes together as a complete idea.

☁️ iCloud Backup

Recordings and notes auto-sync to iCloud. Change your phone without losing anything.

🔒 Privacy First

No account required. Zero data collection. No ads, no trackers.
